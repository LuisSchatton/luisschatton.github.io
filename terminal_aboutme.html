About Me

Hi! I am Luis Schatton. I love 3D Visualization, Virtual Spaces and emerging technologies. I am currently studying communication design at the ‘Hochschule für Gestaltung’ In Schwäbisch Gmünd.

In my work, I like to focus on pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. Experimentation and learning are what keeps me going and drives me to create. The future is full of wonderful opportunities, and I love to explore what’s possible and what could be coming.

To achieve this, I work with a suite of programs. I use Nomad Sculpt for sculpting 3D Objects, ProCreate for Texturing and concept art and Blender for animation, rigging and more. To get them online, I like to work with WebGl using the three.js library. I also have some experience in Unity and SparkAR from working on a variety of Augmented Reality projects. Through my education, I am also very familiar with the Adobe Suite and Figma.
